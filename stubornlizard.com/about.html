About Us!

Hi! Welcome to Stuborn Lizard Quilting! We are a small company that makes personalized quilts for our customers. We can make them completely customizable or we can take all creative liberty. Whatever you need we can do it! Quilting is a traditional way of sewing that goes way back in history that has had cultural signifigance over the years. As time goes by we risk loosing the crafts of the past, so we are here to keep them alive. For any questions contact us!
